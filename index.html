Manav Gupta

Having spent my formative years in a bustling megacity, my first encounter with the Milky Way galaxy gracing the night sky left an indelible mark and ignited a profound passion for space. Working alongside my dad to help settle insurance claims from natural disasters, I discovered the power space technology has to meaningfully impact people on Earth.

As an electronics and communications engineer with a masters in space studies, I specialize in applying earth observation data for downstream agricultural and climate products, where I have gained significant experience in crop detection and flood mapping using advanced machine learning techniques. I am dedicated to working on solutions related to food security and climate risk.
