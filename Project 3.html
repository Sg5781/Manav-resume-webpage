FORECASTING SOLAR IRRADIANCE FOR SOLAR FARMS IN RAJASTHAN

Renewable energy is gaining prominence worldwide due to the depletion of natural reserves and their harmful effects, which are triggering global climate change.

Solar energy is the fastest-growing sector, contributing to more than two-thirds of the growth. However, solar energy output is often affected by unfavorable weather conditions. Scheduling, generation, and the efficiency of photovoltaic farms can benefit from advance knowledge of estimated solar irradiance.

To provide a short-term estimate, deep learning techniques like Long Short Term Memory (LSTM) were employed, using various weather and solar parameters in a time series. The system's efficiency was enhanced through hyperparameter optimization using various 'bio-inspired' techniques."
