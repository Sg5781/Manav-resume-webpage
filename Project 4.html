ASTRONOMY PROJECTS

I have been obsessed with space since high school and I here are some of my projects in the field of astronomy using deep learning.

PREDICTING MISSING DIAMETER VALUES FOR ASTEROIDS

Our solar system is filled with millions of asteroids which were formed during its genesis. Cataloguing them is advantageous to detect another extension level impact on Earth or mine them for resources in the future. The NASA JPL repository has datasets of various asteroids observed through different missions. Some of the missing parameters like the diameter can be predicted based on other features collected. LSTM is used to predict these missing values.

PREDICTING SPECTRAL CLASS OF A STAR

This project determines the spectral class of a star (O,B,A,F,G,K,,M) which is based on the size and heat of star according to the H-R diagram. With the help of other features containing different properties of a star, LSTM is employed to classify the star using the given set of data.
